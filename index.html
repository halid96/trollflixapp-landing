// 14 — faq

Quick answers.

The short version before you get whitelisted.

What happens when I get whitelisted?

Our team reviews your social profiles and checks the kind of content you create. If you make original meme content or understand basic video editing, we'll reach out with a creator partnership request.

What happens when I partner with Trollflix?

Partner creators unlock more than just a profile. Depending on the fit, that can mean access to monetisation tools, private creator opportunities, stronger account presence, and early perks built for people we want close to the platform.

What does invite-only mean?

We review applications manually so the early community stays focused. If your profile fits, we'll contact you from info@trollflix.com.

Do I need a huge audience?

No. Strong timing, originality, and editing taste matter more than follower count. Early creators can grow while the creator pool is still small.

What are MemeJams?

MemeJams are themed creator events with original-only entries, featured placement, and rewards. Think of them as fast, internet-native challenges. Or alternatively means Meme Challenges or Meme competitions.

Can editors join too?

Yes. Editors are part of the ecosystem. If you can make clips sharper, faster, and funnier, there is room for you here.

Is the app live?

The website is live, and the Android and iOS apps are available on Google Play and App Store. You can find official links in the footer.
